Finding the nth term

Finding the nth term can be extremely frustrating.
Basically, let's say you have find the nth term of
6,11,16,21. Goes up by 5 everytime doesn't it?
So we have 5n so far. Hey! You know the first
number in the sequence? Yeah 6. Take 6 away
from 5. (1). So it is 5n + 1

Ratios

Let's say you gotta split 63 into the ratio 3:4. Add 3 and 4. (7). Now divide 63 into 7 (9). Now do 9 * 3 (27) and 9 * 4 (36). So the ratio is 27:36.
